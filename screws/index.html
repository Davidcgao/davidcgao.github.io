SHAKING SCREWS

A dance and sound performance that used recorded and synthesized sounds which were played live, as well as gyroscope data from the dancer's movements which were translated into sound realtime. Performed at the UCLA Broad Art Center EDA, Dec. 7, 2016. Sound: David Gao, Ho Man Leung, Alan Phan Dance: Emily Goldman, Lucas Santhon, Jessie Real Videography: Jesse Fleming, Emil Chang
