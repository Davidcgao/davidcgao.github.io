STILL LIFE (2016) is an interactive audio and visual experience that is affected by the presence of others. Arduino connected sensors on the installation trigger and modulate sound and visuals driven in Max MSP and MadMapper.

This installation was exhibited at Gray Area Art + Tech in San Francisco on Nov. 30th 2016 and Dec 1st, 2016.

Thanks to the artists, educators and community at Gray Area for the support and learning.
