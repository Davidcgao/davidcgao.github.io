ABOUT



VISUAL ARTIST / MOTION DESIGNER BASED IN LA

I have been drawing and playing instruments since the age of five. Visual art was the way in which I expressed myself and music is what kept my scattered mind focused. Naturally, this led me to the music and media art industry. I strive to develop myself as a visual and media artist, while bringing these studio elements to the music industry. I hope to create visual elements that seamlessly and meaningfully tie in with music.
